Homely: Assess what drives your home's value

Created by UC Berkeley MIDS Data Scientists: Louis Yeung, Qiong Zhang, Mohammed Elzubeir

We're buildng a transparent home valuation tool that helps buyers and sellers understand what a home is worth -- and why. Unlike other black-box platforms, our product breaks down how features contribute to a property's value. You can also personalize your preferences, explore "What-if" scenarios, and gain confidence in one of life's biggest financial decisions. Our goal is to make home pricing clear, fair, and interactive. Now, Let's start our journey by entering the first address below!
